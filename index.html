Where a smart parking system greets the University of the West Indies, Mona Campus, staff, students and Visitors

Description

By leveraging technology and data-driven approaches, CampusLots seeks to streamline the parking process for Students, Staff and visitors, thereby reducing the amount of time drivers spend searching for parking spaces.


This reduction in search time will not only enhance the overall experience for drivers, when visiting the campus, but will also contribute to a significant reduction in carbon emissions associated with unnecessary fuel consumption.


This project also integrates features such as Updates on Parking Availability in real-time, intelligent Parking suggestions based on Multi-criteria decision-making, customizable user preferences, and navigation assistance.
Ultimately, these features work hand in hand to ensure our application successully fulfills the needs of its user.
